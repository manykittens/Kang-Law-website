About Us

At Kang Law Practice, we are committed to providing straightforward, accessible, and practical legal solutions tailored to your needs. Based in Cherrybrook and serving the surrounding areas of Hills, Epping, Eastwood, and Norwest, we take pride in delivering quality legal services with a personal touch.

We understand that navigating legal matters can be overwhelming, and our aim is to guide you every step of the way. Whether you’re buying a property, managing family matters, or planning for the future, we are here to help you achieve the best possible outcome with clarity and confidence.

Our Legal Services

Property Law

We provide comprehensive support for all property-related matters, including buying or selling homes, investment properties, or off-the-plan developments. With experience working with individual buyers, sellers, and developers, we understand the nuances of property transactions. From contract reviews and negotiations to settlement, we ensure that the process is seamless and secure. We also advise on strata issues, zoning laws, and conveyancing for residential and commercial properties.

Family Law

Family legal matters require sensitivity and a clear approach. We assist with property settlements, divorce proceedings, parenting arrangements, and consent orders. Our focus is on helping clients navigate these challenging situations with empathy and professionalism. Whether through mediation or court proceedings, we work to achieve practical outcomes that support your family’s well-being and stability.

Commercial Law

Our commercial law services cover business purchases and sales, company structuring, franchise agreements, and lease negotiations. We guide clients through the complexities of contracts, compliance, and regulatory obligations. For retail and commercial leases, we ensure that the terms protect your interests while maintaining clear and fair agreements for all parties involved.

Wills, Estates, and Probate

Planning for the future is essential. We help clients draft tailored wills, establish enduring powers of attorney, and manage probate processes. For executors and beneficiaries, we provide clear guidance on estate administration, ensuring that assets are distributed according to the deceased’s wishes. If disputes arise, we work to resolve them efficiently while minimizing stress for all involved.
